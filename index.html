üéØ Final Conclusion

The Bangladesh Bank heist demonstrates that even central banks are vulnerable to sophisticated nation-state attacks. Comprehensive cybersecurity implementation would have cost significantly less than the $81 million loss. This case underscores the imperative for financial institutions to maintain robust, adaptive security postures in an increasingly connected global ecosystem.

The next attack is not "if" but "when" and "how sophisticated."
